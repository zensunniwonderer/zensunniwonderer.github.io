Locations

Where can you get the freshest fruit near you?

Farmers Market

In a farmers' market, a group of farmers sell their products once or twice a week at a designated public place like a park or parking lot. Some farmers' markets have live entertainment. Shopping at a farmers' market is a great way to meet local farmers and get fresh, flavorful fruit. Use our map to find one near you!

Grocery Store

In the grocery store, you can get most variaty fruit from all diffrent area.

U Pick

Most farms are open on a seasonal basis. Keep in mind that weather conditions and customer demand can determine the supply of fruits and vegetables. Before visiting a farm, call the farm directly to confirm availability, as well as days and hours of operation. I bet you will get most freshest your favorite fruit here than any other place!
